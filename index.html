Planet Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are 4 planets below. By clicking on a planet, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the randome number.


The value of each planet is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game restarts, the game will change the value of each planet.
